Go raibh maith agat as cuairt a thabhairt ar mo gréasáin - Thanks for visiting my website.

Is mise Éanna Ó Ceallaigh. Is innealtóir Éireannach mé.

If you've never heard of a word of the Irish language before, don't worry. It's easy once you know the rules.

My name is Éanna - ee-ah-na - like the name Ian with an ahh at the end.

Ó Ceallaigh - oh-kah-lee - is the original Irish version of Kelly or O'Kelly.

I'm just an engineer - innealtóir - writing about technology related problems I've solved in my work and personal life, mainly focusing on Azure and Kubernetes.
